Spider Plants

Known as Chlorophytum comosum, is the LeBron of house plants. This athlete thrives on low to bright indirect light conditions. It is also on NASA's list of air purifying plants - meaning they help remove toxins like fromaldahyde from the air. And, the best part, they are pet friendly! So your sweet pup or kitten won't suffer for your love of plants.


They also come in a few different varieties!

  • Varigated
  • Reverse
  • Zebra
  • Bonnie
  • Hawaiian
  • Chlorophytum Capense
  • Chlorophytum Bichetii
